At sentencing, the United States District Court clearly articulated that it was denying Ramon De La Cruz's ยง 3B1.2 request because each of his minor-role arguments were contradicted by Zeba Williams' live sentencing testimony that he did not recruit Ramon De La Cruz to commit the offenses and that, instead, Ramon De La Cruz and Zeba Williams entered a joint partnership to smuggle aliens. This explanation alone was sufficient for the United States District Court to find that Ramon De La Cruz's involvement in the offenses was that of an average participant and that Ramon De La Cruz was not any less culpable than Zeba Williams. We hold that the United States District Court's reliance on Zeba Williams' live sentencing testimony as opposed to Raymond McMillon's unsupported arguments was not clearly erroneous. See.
