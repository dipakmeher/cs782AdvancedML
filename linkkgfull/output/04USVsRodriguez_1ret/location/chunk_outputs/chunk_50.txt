The undersigned therefore concludes that not only was the "scope of the traffic stop and the nature of the interrogation" within constitutional bounds, but that the subsequent arrest of IGNACIO RODRIGUEZ, JR. was supported by probable cause. 33 Accordingly, the Magistrate Judge RECOMMENDS that the District Judge, after an independent review, DENY Defendant IGNACIO RODRIGUEZ, JR.'s Motion to Suppress.

III. CONCLUSION

As indicated above, the Magistrate Judge RECOMMENDS that the District Judge, after an independent review, DENY Defendants RICARDO RODRIGUEZ, JR. and IGNACIO RODRIGUEZ, JR.'s Motions to Suppress.
