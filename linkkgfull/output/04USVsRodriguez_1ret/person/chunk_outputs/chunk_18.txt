At  "approximately 9:45 p.m.," Marco Montemayor, Special Agent  for the United States Department of Homeland Security, received a  phone call in Laredo regarding an "attempted alien  smuggling event." ( Id. at 2:01, 2:14 p.m.). He arrived at the Freer checkpoint around an  hour later. (May 3rd Hr'g at 2:14 p.m.). After spending five to ten  minutes speaking with "the primary Border Patrol agent that  conducted the initial stop" to ascertain background  information, Agent Montemayor and his partner interviewed Ricardo  Rodriguez before interviewing Ignacio Rodriguez at "approximately  11:30 p.m." ( Id. at 2:02, 2:10-2:11, 2:14 p.m.). Before the interview, Ignacio  Rodriguez was "inside the Border Patrol station in a holding  cell," behind a locked sliding door. ( Id. at 2:10-2:11 p.m.). Agent Montemayor, along with another HSI agent  and a Border Patrol intel officer, then interviewed Ignacio  Rodriguez in a "fairly large" room. ( Id. at 2:36-2:37 p.m.). No one was in uniform, and their weapons were  locked in a "separate area." ( Id. at 2:18, 2:37 p.m.).
