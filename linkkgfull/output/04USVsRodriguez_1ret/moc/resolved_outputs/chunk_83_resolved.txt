31 Although United States Border Patrol Agent Hugo Benavides testified that he "wasn't sure" if he could have arrested IGNACIO RODRIGUEZ, JR., (May 3rd Hr'g at 1:53-1:54 p.m.), "the subjective belief of an individual officer as to whether there was probable cause for making an arrest is not dispositive," , as probable cause is measured against an objective standard. Furthermore, "even if the decision to place the defendant in the holding cells constituted a de facto arrest, probable cause warranted that arrest."
