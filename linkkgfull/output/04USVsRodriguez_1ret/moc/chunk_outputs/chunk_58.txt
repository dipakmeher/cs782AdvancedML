(May 3rd Hr'g at 1:29 p.m.). United States Border Patrol Agent Hugo Benavides's supervisor had also informed him that United States Border Patrol Agent Jorge Luis Trevino "had encountered a 'LIS,' the code for smuggling aliens, in the car," and that the driver, RICARDO RODRIGUEZ, JR., had been arrested. (May 3rd Hr'g at 1:31-1:32 p.m.). United States Border Patrol Agent Jorge Luis Trevino had encountered the illegal aliens only after running "another license plate, for a black Dodge pickup truck" that "kept going straight towards U.S. Highway 359" after United States Border Patrol Agent Jorge Luis Trevino pulled over the white Malibu sedan. (May 3rd Hr'g at 1:29 p.m.). United States Border Patrol Agent Jorge Luis Trevino had therefore radioed for United States Border Patrol agents, and particularly United States Border Patrol Agent Hugo Benavides because of his location on Highway 16, "to be on the lookout for that type of a truck." (May 3rd Hr'g at 1:30 p.m.).
