Defense counsel cross-examined Bruce Henderson regarding the investigation.  Bruce Henderson stated that although there were only two seats in the cab, Claudia may have sat in the bunk. Bruce Henderson reiterated that Martin Adaboh's statement was taken five hours after Yusif Ahmed Rufied's statement was taken, and that at the time of arriving at the scene only Yusif Ahmed Rufied and not Martin Adaboh spoke to agents. Bruce Henderson also reiterated that Yusif Ahmed Rufied stated that Martin Adaboh spoke with a lady by the name of Claudia and that Yusif Ahmed Rufied stated that he slept in the tractor trailer. Bruce Henderson testified that one material witness identified Martin Adaboh as the driver and the person that was present at the rear of the tractor trailer while the tractor trailer was being loaded with aliens. However, Bruce Henderson also stated that the other material witnesses identified a person with black skin color as the person loading the tractor trailer. Bruce Henderson testified that there were no justifiable facts that the $1700 found in Yusif Ahmed Rufied's possession was from this incident. Bruce Henderson reiterated that Martin Adaboh never mentioned that Yusif Ahmed Rufied was getting paid. Bruce Henderson reiterated that the primary agent on the scene informed Bruce Henderson that Yusif Ahmed Rufied was in the passenger seat of the tractor trailer. Other than the cross-examination, Yusif Ahmed Rufied did not present any additional testimony as to probable cause.
