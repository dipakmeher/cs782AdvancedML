Agent Bruce Henderson testified that Martin Adaboh described the area surrounding Diesel Impact 2000 and could show Bruce Henderson where it was located. Bruce Henderson stated that Martin Adaboh did not state the exact amount of the payment he was going to receive for transporting the aliens and never claimed that he was going to pay Yusif Ahmed Rufied. Bruce Henderson stated that Martin Adaboh stated that he started with $10,000, gave $3000 to his wife, gave Yusif Ahmed Rufied $1000, and Martin Adaboh was left with $7,000 found at the time he was arrested.

Bruce Henderson testified that Yusif Ahmed Rufied claimed that they were going to McAllen, Texas; Martin Adaboh claimed that they were going to San Antonio, Texas; and the trucking dispatcher stated that Yusif Ahmed Rufied and Martin Adaboh were to pick up a load in Laredo, Texas and return to Canada. Bruce Henderson testified that Martin Adaboh stated that Yusif Ahmed Rufied was awake in the tractor trailer when they went to Diesel Impact 2000. Bruce Henderson also stated that there was no load in the tractor trailer, except for the aliens, when Yusif Ahmed Rufied was stopped at the United States Border Patrol check point on Highway 59. Bruce Henderson testified that a person does not travel on Highway 59 to get to San Antonio or McAllen.
