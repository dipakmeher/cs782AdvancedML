At the preliminary examination hearing, the government presented Immigration and Customs Enforcement ("ICE") Agent Bruce Henderson ("Agent Henderson") as its sole witness. Bruce Henderson testified that on May 24, 2006, he was working with Joe Silva when they were called to the Border Patrol check point on Highway 59. Bruce Henderson stated that Joe Silva received a duty call from Flores stating that sixty (60) undocumented aliens were found in a tractor trailer. Bruce Henderson testified that he arrived at the Border Patrol check point on Highway 59 with Joe Silva at approximately 10:45 a.m. Bruce Henderson stated that when he arrived at the Border Patrol check point on Highway 59 he saw the tractor trailer and was introduced to some of the undocumented aliens found in the trailer. Bruce Henderson stated that there were fifty-nine (59) aliens present at the Border Patrol check point on Highway 59 and one (1) alien in the hospital due to taking an improper medication. Bruce Henderson testified that Border Patrol Agents advised him that the tractor trailer arrived at the Border Patrol check point on Highway 59 at approximately 6:45 a.m. and during a secondary inspection sixty (60) aliens were found in the rear of the trailer.
