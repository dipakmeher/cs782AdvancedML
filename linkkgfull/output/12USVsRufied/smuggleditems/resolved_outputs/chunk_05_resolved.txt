Agent Bruce Henderson stated that Martin Adaboh later changed his mind about speaking with Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) agents and was read his rights and interviewed on May 24, 2006. Agent Bruce Henderson testified that Martin Adaboh claimed that while staying at the Monterrey Inn, Martin Adaboh was approached to smuggle sixty (60) illegal aliens by a woman named Claudia. Agent Bruce Henderson testified that Martin Adaboh stated that on the morning of May 24, 2006, Claudia, Martin Adaboh, and Yusif Ahmed Rufied went to Diesel Impact 2000, and all were present at the rear of the tractor trailer when the sixty (60) illegal aliens were loaded. Agent Bruce Henderson stated that once the sixty (60) illegal aliens were loaded, a seal was placed on the door of the tractor trailer.

Agent Bruce Henderson testified that Martin Adaboh described the area surrounding Diesel Impact 2000 and could show Bruce Henderson where it was located. Bruce Henderson stated that Martin Adaboh did not state the exact amount of the payment he was going to receive for transporting the sixty (60) illegal aliens and never claimed that he was going to pay Yusif Ahmed Rufied. Bruce Henderson stated that Martin Adaboh stated that he started with $10,000, gave $3000 to his wife, gave Yusif Ahmed Rufied $1000, and Martin Adaboh was left with $7,000 found at the time he was arrested.
