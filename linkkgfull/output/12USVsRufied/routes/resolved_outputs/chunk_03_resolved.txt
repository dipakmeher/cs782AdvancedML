Agent Bruce Henderson testified that Yusif Ahmed Rufied was in the cab of the tractor trailer with Martin Adaboh ("Codefendant"). Agent Bruce Henderson identified Yusif Ahmed Rufied as the individual in the Southern District of Texas wearing an orange jumpsuit. Agent Bruce Henderson stated that Yusif Ahmed Rufied and Martin Adaboh are both from Ghana, Africa and both live in Quebec, Montreal, Canada. Agent Bruce Henderson testified that Martin Adaboh and Yusif Ahmed Rufied were arrested and read their rights. Agent Bruce Henderson stated that he attempted to speak with Martin Adaboh first, but was unable to speak with Martin Adaboh because Martin Adaboh requested an attorney. Agent Bruce Henderson testified that he spoke with Yusif Ahmed Rufied, and Yusif Ahmed Rufied claimed that he was a driver in training, that he never drove the tractor trailer, and that he was not receiving payment for transporting aliens.
