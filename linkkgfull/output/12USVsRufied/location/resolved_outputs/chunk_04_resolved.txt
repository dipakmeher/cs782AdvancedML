Agent Bruce Henderson spoke with material witnesses that claimed to be loaded into the trailer at 5:30 a.m. on May 24, 2006, during the period Yusif Ahmed Rufied claims he was sleeping in the tractor.

Agent Bruce Henderson testified that the aliens were loaded at Diesel Impact 2000 off of Highway 359. Agent Bruce Henderson described the terrain leading into Diesel Impact 2000 as a rough caliche road, with a steep grade, and deep indentations. Agent Bruce Henderson testified that Yusif Ahmed Rufied claimed the tractor stopped on the side of the highway and that Martin Adaboh told Yusif Ahmed Rufied he was checking the tires.

Agent Bruce Henderson stated that Martin Adaboh later changed his mind about speaking with ICE agents and was read his rights and interviewed on May 24, 2006. Agent Bruce Henderson testified that Martin Adaboh claimed that while staying at the Monterrey Inn, Martin Adaboh was approached to smuggle aliens by a woman named Claudia. Agent Bruce Henderson testified that Martin Adaboh stated that on the morning of May 24, 2006, Claudia, Martin Adaboh, and Yusif Ahmed Rufied went to Diesel Impact 2000, and all were present at the rear of the trailer when the aliens were loaded. Agent Bruce Henderson stated that once the aliens were loaded, a seal was placed on the door of the trailer.
