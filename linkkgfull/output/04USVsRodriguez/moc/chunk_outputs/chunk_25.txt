The analysis begins with inquiring into the black Dodge pickup truck's proximity to the United States-Mexico border. Proximity to the United States-Mexico border has been deemed both a 'paramount factor' and a 'vital element' in determining reasonable suspicion, because proximity suggests that the black Dodge pickup truck in fact originated its journey at the United States-Mexico border and is thus more likely to harbor illegal aliens.
