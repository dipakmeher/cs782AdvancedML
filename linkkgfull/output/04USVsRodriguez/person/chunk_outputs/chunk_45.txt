The  undersigned therefore concludes that not only was the "scope of  the traffic stop and the nature of the interrogation" within  constitutional bounds, but that the subsequent arrest of Ignacio  Rodriguez was supported by probable cause. 33 Accordingly, the Magistrate Judge RECOMMENDS that the District  Judge, after an independent review, DENY Defendant Ignacio  Rodriguez's Motion to Suppress.

III.  CONCLUSION

As  indicated above, the Magistrate Judge RECOMMENDS that the District  Judge, after an independent review, DENY Defendant Ricardo  Rodriguez's and Ignacio Rodriguez's Motions to Suppress.
