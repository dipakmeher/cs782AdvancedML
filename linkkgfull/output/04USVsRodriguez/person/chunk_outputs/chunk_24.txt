The  analysis begins with inquiring into the vehicle's proximity to the  border. Proximity to the border has been deemed both a "paramount  factor" and a "vital element" in determining  reasonable suspicion, because proximity suggests that the vehicle in  fact originated its journey at the border and is thus more likely to  harbor illegal aliens.
