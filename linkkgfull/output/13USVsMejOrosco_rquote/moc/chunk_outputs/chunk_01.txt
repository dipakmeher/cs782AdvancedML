Juventino Mejia-Orosco is a defendant.

CLARK, District Judge of the Western District of Louisiana:

Juventino Mejia-Orosco was convicted of illegally transporting aliens into the United States of America in violation of. He does not contest the underlying conviction, but argues that the United States District Court imposed an improper sentence. Because the United States District Court correctly applied the United States Sentencing Commission Guidelines to its factual findings, and because those findings were not clearly erroneous, the United States Court of Appeals affirm.

I.

Juventino Mejia-Orosco is a citizen of Guatemala. He earns his living trucking curios from Guatemala, through Mexico, to the United States of America. In April of 1988, on one of his many trips to this country, he brought along Hector Galvencio Paz-Ortega and Carlos Enrique Cruz-Morales, both citizens of Guatemala and purportedly related to Juventino Mejia-Orosco. After Juventino Mejia-Orosco and his two passengers had travelled through Mexico and arrived in Nuevo Laredo, Tamaulipas, Mexico, Hector Galvencio Paz-Ortega and Carlos Enrique Cruz-Morales disembarked.
