David Fitzgerald cited the modification to the modified backseat of the crew-cab truck used by Carlos Martinez-Romero as another articulated reason for conducting the stop. Vehicle Registration

Prior to the stop, United States Border Patrol Agent David Fitzgerald ran a license and vehicle registration check on the crew-cab truck used by Carlos Martinez-Romero. United States Border Patrol Agent David Fitzgerald discovered the crew-cab truck used by Carlos Martinez-Romero was registered in Edna, Texas. United States Border Patrol Agent David Fitzgerald testified that in his experience, several of the alien-smuggling vehicles he apprehended in the past originated from Edna, a suburb of Dallas.
