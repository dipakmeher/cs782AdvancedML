And while it is questionable whether the large white Ford pickup truck's five-second pause at the stop sign and adherence to the school zone speed limit were especially suspicious, the large white Ford pickup truck's indirect travel route from Big Bend National Park to Alpine, Texas does trigger suspicion. See (approving the U.S. District Court's consideration of an indirect route as a factor supporting reasonable suspicion). This circuit affords due weight to the inferences United States/Mexican Border Patrol draw from historical facts and the events leading up to a stop. Id. Considering the totality of the driver's behavior, this factor weighs in favor of reasonable suspicion.
