b. Upward adjustment for weapon possession

Brignoni-Ponce Jacquinot also contends that the U.S. District Court erred in applying Brignoni-Ponce Jacquinot's two-level upward adjustment for possession of a weapon in connection with a drug trafficking offense. Brignoni-Ponce Jacquinot argues that although there were two unloaded handguns in the large white Ford pickup truck, the United States Government failed to prove that it was not clearly improbable that Brignoni-Ponce Jacquinot possessed those handguns in connection with Brignoni-Ponce Jacquinot's drug offense.
