In the course of effectuating the stop, a Williamson County Sheriff's Office officer may permissibly examine the driver's license of Federico Saldana and vehicle registration of the 2008 Chevrolet truck driven by Federico Saldana and run a computer checks run by Williamson County Sheriff's Office officer check on them to investigate whether the driver has any outstanding warrants and if the vehicle is stolen.
