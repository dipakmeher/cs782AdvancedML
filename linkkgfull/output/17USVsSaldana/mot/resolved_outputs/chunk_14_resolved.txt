Here, Gary Haston testified he observed Federico Saldana having trouble maintaining his 2008 Chevrolet truck and flatbed trailer carrying a piece of heavy machinery in a single lane, and that Federico Saldana was changing lanes in an unsafe manner resulting in two near-collisions. Gary Haston directly relayed this information to Jason Cox, and Jason Cox proceeded to locate Federico Saldana and conduct a traffic stop. Therefore, Jason Cox had an objectively reasonable suspicion Federico Saldana had committed multiple traffic violations as witnessed by Gary Haston and Heather Vargas. In addition, Jason Cox, upon locating Federico Saldana's 2008 Chevrolet truck and flatbed trailer, witnessed the flatbed trailer veering onto Interstate 35 before he initiated the stop.
