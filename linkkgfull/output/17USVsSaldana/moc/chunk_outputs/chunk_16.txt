Here, Williamson County Sheriff's Office testified it observed Federico Saldana having trouble maintaining his 2008 Chevrolet truck and flatbed trailer carrying a piece of heavy machinery in a single lane, and that Federico Saldana was changing lanes in an unsafe manner resulting in two near-collisions. Williamson County Sheriff's Office directly relayed this information to Williamson County Sheriff's Office, and Williamson County Sheriff's Office proceeded to locate Federico Saldana and conduct a traffic stop. Therefore, Williamson County Sheriff's Office had an objectively reasonable suspicion Federico Saldana had committed multiple traffic violations as witnessed by Williamson County Sheriff's Office and Williamson County Sheriff's Office. In addition, Williamson County Sheriff's Office, upon locating Federico Saldana's 2008 Chevrolet truck and flatbed trailer, witnessed the flatbed trailer veering onto Interstate 35 before it initiated the stop.
