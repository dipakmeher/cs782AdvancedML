Here,  Haston testified he observed Saldana having trouble maintaining his  vehicle and the attached trailer carrying a piece of heavy machinery  in a single lane, and that Saldana was changing lanes in an unsafe  manner resulting in two near-collisions. Haston directly relayed this information to Cox, and Cox proceeded  to locate Saldana and conduct a traffic stop. Therefore, Cox had an  objectively reasonable suspicion Saldana had committed multiple  traffic violations as witnessed by Haston and Vargas. In addition,  Cox, upon locating Saldana's truck and trailer, witnessed the  trailer veering onto the shoulder before he initiated the stop.
