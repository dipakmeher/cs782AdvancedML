In  summary, Ibarra-Zelaya has not demonstrated that his attorney's  performance was deficient or that he suffered actual prejudice at  his sentencing. Therefore, Ibarra-Zelaya fails to establish a valid  claim for ineffective assistance of counsel or to show that he is  entitled to relief under on this issue. Because Ibarra-Zelaya has not shown that he is  entitled to relief under on any of the issues he presents in his motion and supplemental  memoranda, the government's motion to dismiss is granted.
