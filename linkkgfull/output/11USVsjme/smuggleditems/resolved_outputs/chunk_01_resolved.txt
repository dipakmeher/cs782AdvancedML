J.M.E. is a defendant.

MEMORANDUM OPINION

AFFIRMED

J.M.E. appeals the Juvenile Court's order committing him to the Texas Juvenile Justice Department for an indeterminate term, not to exceed his nineteenth birthday. On appeal, J.M.E. argues the Juvenile Court abused its discretion because less restrictive alternatives were available and commitment to the Texas Juvenile Justice Department violated his rights. We affirm the Juvenile Court's order.
