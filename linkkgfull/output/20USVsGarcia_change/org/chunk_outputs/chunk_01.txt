TATE, Circuit Judge:

This appeal is brought by Maria De Jesus Garcia, who was convicted by a United States District Court jury of four counts of aiding and abetting the transportation of undocumented aliens in pickup truck camper on Interstate Highway 35 in violation of and. Maria De Jesus Garcia alleges that the United States District Court judge erred in refusing to suppress evidence which she says was obtained by the government as a result of an unconstitutional stop and search of pickup truck camper on Interstate Highway 35. Maria De Jesus Garcia also challenges the United States District Court judge's reading of a modified Allen charge, known as Brignoni-Ponce standard, to the jury, and the judge's refusal to allow post-trial interviews of jurors by defense counsel.
