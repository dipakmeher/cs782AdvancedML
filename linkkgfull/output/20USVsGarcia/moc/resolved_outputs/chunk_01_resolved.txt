GARWOOD, Circuit Judge:

This appeal is brought by Maria De Jesus Garcia, who was convicted by a jury in Maria De Jesus Garcia's trial of four counts of aiding and abetting the transportation of undocumented aliens in violation of and. Maria De Jesus Garcia alleges that the United States District Court judge erred in refusing to suppress evidence which Maria De Jesus Garcia says was obtained by the United States Government as a result of an unconstitutional investigatory stop on Interstate Highway 35 and the search of the pickup truck camper. Maria De Jesus Garcia also challenges the United States District Court judge's reading of a modified Allen charge used in the 5th Circuit Court of Appeals to the jury in Maria De Jesus Garcia's trial, and the judge's refusal to allow post-trial interviews of jurors by defense counsel.
