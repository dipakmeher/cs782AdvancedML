GARWOOD, Circuit Judge:

This appeal is brought by Maria De Jesus Garcia, who was convicted by a jury in the United States District Court trial of four counts of aiding and abetting the transportation of undocumented aliens in violation of and. Maria De Jesus Garcia alleges that the United States District Court judge erred in refusing to suppress evidence which Maria De Jesus Garcia says was obtained by the United States Government as a result of an unconstitutional stop on Interstate Highway 35 and search of pickup truck camper. Maria De Jesus Garcia also challenges the United States District Court judge's reading of a modified Allen charge to the jury in the United States District Court trial, and the judge's refusal to allow post-trial interviews of jurors by defense counsel.
