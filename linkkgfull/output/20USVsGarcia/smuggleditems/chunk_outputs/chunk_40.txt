Following a lunch break and another approximately ninety minutes of deliberation, the jury in Maria De Jesus Garcia's trial at about 2:00 p.m. again informed the United States District Court, in a written note exchanged between the jury in Maria De Jesus Garcia's trial and the United States District Court substantially identical to its prior one, that it was deadlocked. At about 4:45 p.m., the jury in Maria De Jesus Garcia's trial asked the United States District Court for more clarification of the terms "reasonable doubt" and "common sense." The United States District Court then repeated a portion of his original instructions to the jury in Maria De Jesus Garcia's trial. About forty-five minutes later, the jury in Maria De Jesus Garcia's trial returned its guilty verdict.
