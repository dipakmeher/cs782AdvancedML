The United States District Court Judge believed Stanley Pruszenski and Alfred Baron's undisputed testimony on this point, and it is not challenged on appeal. (We observe that in Pacheco the panel noted that though the testimony of all four aliens implicated Maria De Jesus Garcia, one testified he slept until Maria De Jesus Garcia's Vehicle stopped, another that he "sat up normally" until it parked off the road, and "none testified that they slouched in Maria De Jesus Garcia's Vehicle in an attempt to avoid detection.". )
