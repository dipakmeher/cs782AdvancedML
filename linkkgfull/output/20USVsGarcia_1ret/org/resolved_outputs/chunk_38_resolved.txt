Following a lunch break and another approximately ninety minutes of deliberation, the United States District Court Jury at about 2:00 p.m. again informed the United States District Court Judge, in a note substantially identical to its prior one, that it was deadlocked. The United States District Court Judge then, over objections of Maria De Jesus Garcia's counsel and a motion for mistrial, read the modified Allen Charge to the United States District Court Jury. At about 4:45 p.m., the United States District Court Jury asked the United States District Court Judge for more clarification of the terms "reasonable doubt" and "common sense." The United States District Court Judge then repeated a portion of his original instructions to the United States District Court Jury. About forty-five minutes later, the United States District Court Jury returned its guilty verdict.
