GARWOOD, Circuit Judge:

This appeal is brought by Maria De Jesus Garcia, who was convicted by a United States District Court Jury of four counts of aiding and abetting the transportation of undocumented aliens in Maria De Jesus Garcia's Vehicle in violation of and. Maria De Jesus Garcia alleges that the United States District Court Judge erred in refusing to suppress evidence which she says was obtained by the United States Government as a result of an unconstitutional stop of Maria De Jesus Garcia's Vehicle. Maria De Jesus Garcia also challenges the United States District Court Judge's reading of a modified Allen Charge to the United States District Court Jury, and the judge's refusal to allow post-trial interviews of jurors by Maria De Jesus Garcia's defense counsel.
